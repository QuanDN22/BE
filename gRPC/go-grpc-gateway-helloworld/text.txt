Now, on to generating the keys. OpenSSL added ed25519 support in 2017 
so any modern version should support it. 
For our examples, we are going to use the following commands:
openssl genpkey -algorithm ED25519 -outform pem -out auth.ed
openssl pkey -in auth.ed -pubout > auth.ed.pub
